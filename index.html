Definition of Music

• Music is a form of art that involves combining instrumental or vocal sounds in accordance with cultural standards of melody, rhythm and harmony.
Therefore, the definition, creation and performance of music varies widely across cultures.
• Music is an important cultural aspect and plays a key role in cultural practices such as rites of passage, religious rituals and social activities.
The music industry comprises of composers, performers, producers and event organisers among other stakeholders.
