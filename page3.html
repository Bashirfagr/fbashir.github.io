Composing Music

• Music consists of numerous elements among them lyrics, melody and harmony.
In classical music, people who create music are known as composers while in contemporary music they are known as songwriters.
• The order followed in creating harmony and melody varies among composers and songwriters.
The art of music composition requires great talent, experience and following established guidelines to create masterpieces.
Typically, a composition structure consists of the following: introduction, verse, chorus, verse, chorus, bridge, verse, chorus, outro.
