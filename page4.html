Recording, Tracking, and Arranging Music

• Once a composition is written and receives approval from artist and/or producer, recording, tracking and arranging the song takes place.
In arrangement, the producer selects the vocals and instruments to play in each section in the timeline of the song.
• Tracking involves recording the numerous instruments used in the performance of a song.
In this case, vocals and instrumentation are recorded, tracked and arranged for the purpose of fine-tuning music for production.
