Mixing and Editing

Editing entails normal housekeeping tasks such as copying, pasting, cutting, trimming and nudging the different parts of a song as producer and/or artist deems fit.
Other tasks include adjusting pitches and polishing trackings to smoothen the transition between different parts. The focus is to have the music sound as pleasant as possible.
In mixing, the producer puts effects on the final track. Besides, it involves adjusting volume levels,equalisation and rhythm correction to produce a complete and "clean" song.
